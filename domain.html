REFUND AND CANCELLATION POLICY

Introduction At Recools Research Private limited(Cash Urn) , we strive to provide our customers with the best services possible. However, we understand that there may be instances where customers may wish to cancel or request a refund for their transactions. This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds will be granted and the process for requesting a refund.
Refund Policy : In the event that a transaction is cancelled due to technical difficulties, we will process a full refund to the customer.
Cancellation Policy : Customers may cancel their transaction at any time prior to the completion of the transaction. In the event of a cancellation, any funds that have been processed will be returned to the customer.
Refund Process : To request a refund, customers should contact us at support@cashurn.com. We will review the request and process the refund within 3 days.
Disputes : In the event of a dispute regarding a refund or cancellation, we will work with the customer to resolve the issue in a fair and timely manner.
Changes to this Policy : We may update this Refund and Cancellation Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or in applicable laws. Any changes will be posted on this page and the effective date of the updated policy will be indicated.
